Meet Taylor

Taylor is a safety instructor with experience teaching CPR, BLS, childcare health and safety, emergency preparedness, and wilderness response concepts.

```

Relatable. Practical. Real.

Taylor’s strength is making serious information easy to understand and hard to forget. He adapts his teaching style to the group in front of him — whether that means helping childcare providers stay engaged through a long licensing course, walking healthcare workers through BLS skills, or preparing outdoor groups for emergencies in remote environments.


His classes are built around real-world examples, humor, clear expectations, and practical scenarios that help students feel more confident when it counts.
```
